About Us

Welcome to Soulsync Physiotherapy Hub - where compassionate care meets clinical excellence. We specialize in restoring movement, relieving pain, and helping our patients regain their quality of life through evidence-based physiotherapy treatments.

Personalized Physiotherapy Care for Every Patient.

From everyday aches to complex neurological rehabilitation, our experienced physiotherapists design treatment plans tailored to your goals - whether that is returning to sport, walking pain-free, or recovering from surgery.

  • Specialty: Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation
  • Approach: Evidence-Based, Patient-First
  • Modalities: Manual Therapy, Electrotherapy, Exercise
  • Home Visits: Available on Request
  • Consultation: By Appointment
  • Languages: English, Hindi, Urdu

At Soulsync, we believe healing is a partnership. Our team listens carefully, assesses thoroughly, and treats with the latest techniques in musculoskeletal, neurological, paediatric and geriatric physiotherapy. Whether you are managing chronic pain or recovering from a stroke, we are here to guide you - step by step - back to a stronger, healthier you.

Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive physiotherapy care for orthopaedic, neurological, paediatric and geriatric conditions.

Pain Management

Neck Pain, Back Pain, Knee Pain, Heel Pain, Joint Pain, Sciatica and Plantar Fasciitis - relief through targeted manual therapy and rehabilitation.

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Slip Disc, Frozen Shoulder, Arthritis, Posture Correction, Muscle Weakness, Sports Injuries and Pre & Post Surgery Rehabilitation.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Stroke, Paralysis, Hemiplegia, Facial Palsy, Parkinson's, Wrist Drop, Foot Drop and Guillain-Barre Syndrome rehabilitation.

Paediatric & Geriatric Care

Cerebral Palsy, Polio Rehabilitation, Paediatric Rehabilitation and Geriatric Rehabilitation tailored for children and seniors.

Women's Health

Pre and Post Natal Rehabilitation, Postpartum Rehabilitation - supporting mothers through every stage of pregnancy and recovery.

Lifestyle & Wellness

Weight Loss and Weight Gain Rehabilitation programs combining exercise therapy, nutritional guidance and lifestyle coaching.
